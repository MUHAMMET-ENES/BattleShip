BATTLESHIP

TACTICAL WARFARE

Developer: Muhammet Enes Karaoğlan

HOW TO PLAY

Objective

Sink all enemy ships! Destroying the Carrier (5-unit) grants an instant victory.

Controls & Placement

Drag or click to place ships. Press 'R' key or use the on-screen 'Rotate' button to change orientation.

Game Modes

  • PvC: Race against the Tactical AI.
  • PvP: Pass-and-play with a friend.

Scoring

Hit: +10 Pts | Sink: +10 Bonus Pts. Leaderboard is ranked by fewest moves first.
